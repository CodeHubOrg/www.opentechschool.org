We organise our work in teams. Each team is responsible for certain areas of the OpenTechSchool.

Teams

OpenTechSchool Global Public discussions about OpenTechSchool interesting the movement on a global level. Team Stockholm OTS Stockholm team members in Stockholm and anyone connected with and interested in it. JavaScript Coaches Coaches collaborating on new learning material and setting up new JS-dedicated events. Python Coaches Python coaches and organizers talking about learning material and upcoming events. Git Coaches Coaches and OTS coordinators involved in OpenTechSchool's Git series. Arduino Coaches Arduino Coaches talking about learning material and planning new workshops. Math-3d Coaches Coaches responsible for workshops dedicated to math for visuals and design. Design Coaches Design coaches discussing about learning material and upcoming events. Team Beginners Meetup Beginners Meetup team members and anyone interested in proposing new talks for future events. Team Online Courses OTS team members organising events around online courses such as Coursera and Udacity. Team Tech OTS' tech and administrative team. Team Communications Team to OTS members discussing about and taking care of external communications and marketing realationships. Team Content Content creators responsible for all kinds of copy and texts. Team Blueprint OTS members working on blueprints for new OTS chapters. Team Sponsors The right place to talk about sponsorship opportunities and offer your support to OTS.
